INSTRUCTION

Role: Normal user

Step 1: The conversation is initiated by you. You can select 'intent' and enter the information in the response, click 'send'. To send the information continuously, click 'send another message'.

Step 2: After successfully sending, please wait for the system user to reply or recommend the product to you. If you think your goal has not been achieved, you can repeat step one to continue the conversation; On the contrary, you can click finish the conversation.

Step 3: After the conversation, you can evaluate the relevance of the products recommended by the system users, and also evaluate the system users.

Role: System

Step 1: After waiting for the user to send the first message, you can select the corresponding action and make a response.

Step 2: When you feel that you have understood the user's intention, you can enter query and search. After successful search, you can show filters and search for more accurate products based on the filters.

Step 3: When you believe that there are already products that meet the user's needs, you can select 'recommend' in the action and select the corresponding product to recommend to the user in the search results bar.

Step 4: After the user clicks finish the conversation, you can also evaluate the conversation.
